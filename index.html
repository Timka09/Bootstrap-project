Ultralight spinning

This is the lightest type of spinning rod, which is usually used for catching small fish. It has a length of 1.5 to 2.1 meters and is usually used with a line that has a thickness of 2 to 6 pounds.

Match rod

This rod is equipped with a light spinning reel with an increased winding speed, which allows you to manipulate the equipment very quickly, and if necessary, catch very large trophies, while using thin lines and small hooks. The float should be clearly visible and have a streamlined shape for long-distance casting.

Medium spinning

This is a versatile type of spinning rod that can be used to catch most types of fish. It has a length of 2.1 to 3.0 meters and is usually used with a line that has a thickness of 6 to 12 pounds.

Plug rod

This is a special fishing tool, previously available only to sport fishermen, it allows fishing with a dead tackle at a long distance, without using a reel. At the same time, very precise baiting is practiced, for this there are special baiting scoops. The rod itself is relatively heavy, this is also important to remember, to fish with it you need to have some skill.

Heavy spinning

This is the most powerful type of spinning rod and is usually used for catching large fish. It has a length of 2.4 to 3.6 meters and is usually used with a line that is 10 to 20 pounds thick.

Float rod

one of the simplest fishing tackles, which is why it is so widespread. A float rod consists of a rod, a fishing line with a float, a sinker, and a hook with bait attached to it. The bait is cast into the water using the rod. The behavior of the float (bite) determines the moment when the fish holds the bait in its mouth. When there is a bite, a hook is set - a small sharp movement of the rod. The hooked fish is pulled out onto the shore. A float rod can be used for fishing close to the angler (winter fishing, fishing from a boat, from bridges, etc.), at a distance of twice the length of the rod (sport fishing), at a distance many times greater than the length of the rod (long-distance casting). A float rod can be used to fish any point in a body of water: from the surface of the water to the bottom and from the shore to a distance of tens of meters.
